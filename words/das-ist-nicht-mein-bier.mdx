---
title: Das ist nicht mein Bier
pubDate: 15/01/2024 16:25
author: "Roman Ponomarev"
---

Literally translates to "This is not my beer", is equivalent to saying "That's not my cup of tea" or "That's not my problem." It's used to indicate that something is outside of one's area of expertise or interest, implying that it's not their concern or issue.
